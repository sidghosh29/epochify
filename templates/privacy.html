Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Epochify handles information when you use this website. Epochify is a simple developer tool for converting Unix timestamps to human-readable dates and back. This site is operated by Siddharth Ghosh.

By using Epochify, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Epochify does not intentionally collect or store personal information. Timestamp conversions are processed in real time on the server and your input is not retained after your request is complete. No user accounts are required to use this site.

3. Cookies and Advertising

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. These cookies allow Google to show you relevant ads across the web.

You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time by visiting Google Ads Settings. You can also opt out through the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.

4. Third-Party Services

Epochify uses the following third-party services, which may collect data independently under their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdSense — for serving advertisements. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — for typography. Fonts are loaded from Google's servers, which may log your IP address and browser type. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Epochify does not control how these services collect or use data.

5. Log Data

Like most web servers, Epochify's hosting infrastructure may automatically record basic log data when you visit the site. This can include your IP address, browser type and version, the pages you visit, and the date and time of your request. This data is used solely for operational purposes such as diagnosing errors and is not linked to any personal identity.

6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in how the site operates or to comply with legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised date at the top.

7. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via the Contact page.
